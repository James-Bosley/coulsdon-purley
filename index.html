About Us

Coulsdon & Purley badminton club has been operating for over 70 years and has more than 200 members of all ages playing at all levels from beginners to county standard. We have teams in multiple county and local leagues. Our badminton hall is purpose built of county standard with 3 courts, high quality lighting and a brand new sprung wood floor.

Competitions

Our club is structured across 5 grades of ability, from 5 for beginnners right up to 1 for county standard players. Each grade has teams which are entered into league competions at both a local level and in Surrey county leagues. We also have an annual in-house grade tournament, culminating in an exiting finals day competition with the whole club invited to attend.
